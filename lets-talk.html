Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our consultation process

What should I prepare for the consultation?

To make the most of your consultation, we recommend preparing a brief overview of your project goals, any specific challenges you're facing, and questions you'd like to discuss. If you have any existing documentation or specifications, having those on hand can be helpful, but it's not required.

Is there any obligation after the consultation?

No, there is absolutely no obligation to proceed with a project after the consultation. Our goal is to provide valuable insights and help you understand your options. You're free to take that information and make the best decision for your business.

How soon can I expect to receive a quote?

For standard projects, we typically provide quotes within 2-3 business days after receiving all necessary information. For more complex projects that require detailed scoping, it may take 5-7 business days. We'll always keep you informed about the timeline for receiving your quote.

Can I change my consultation time after booking?

Yes, you can reschedule your consultation if needed. We ask that you provide at least 24 hours' notice when possible. You'll receive a confirmation email after booking that includes a link to reschedule if necessary.
